This page is intended as a tutorial for using the titan cluster at UMass to perform parallelized simulations with MadGraph and CosmoTransitions. The titan cluster is maintained by Joe Babcock and Ben Brau.

For general questions about running simulations and any errors you see in the following tutorial, please contact me. For general questions about titan, your first point of contact should be Joe. However, if neither Joe or I can resolve your issue, you can also contact Ben.

In the section, I’ll describe the process of getting started with titan.
